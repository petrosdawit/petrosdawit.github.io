Hi, My name is Petros Dawit!

I'm a rising junior pursuing a ScB in computer science at Brown University.
I have a strong passion for technology and building things. This website was built entirely by me with HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, Javascript and PHP.


A little bit about me...

I was born and raised in Virginia but went to an internatinal school called W.I.S. in Washington D.C.. I currently am attending Brown University. I fell in love with computer science my sophomore year after I was influenced by my friends to take the intro course. To me, computer science is empowering. It has the qualities of the sciences and the qualities of craft that I both enjoy and like combining to create something new or to extend my knowledge.

  • I currently am interning at GoodWorld, which is a tech startup in D.C. that is trying to revolutionize the way people give to charities and causes they care about.

  • Things I like: Computer Science, visiting places, soccer, foodie, giving back, music and art!
